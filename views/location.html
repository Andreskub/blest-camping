How to reach Blest Camping?

The best way to get to Blest Camping is by boat. We have a boat service which transports from Puerto Blest to Puerto Pañuelo and vice versa, and they operate throughout the day.

We also offer a free car parking for our customers just a few meters from Puerto Pañuelo where you can leave their car safe and sound.
